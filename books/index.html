Books

I have been reading books for as log as I remember. Some of the books I love are LOTR, ASOIAF, Foundation Saga, Dune series, Discworld. I like books with vast, expansive worlds and gray characters. I also love hard science fiction.

Authors

I like Asimov for his prolificity, I like Martin for introducing realism to fantasy and I like Pratchett for his skill with words.
